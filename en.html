Behind Dark Axolotl

Ton Cordero

Music Producer and Mixing & Mastering Engineer

Full credit list on Muso.AI

Dark Axolotl is a Mexico City based company specialized in music production, stereo and immersive mixing & mastering and other audio services. With more than 531k streams in 2025 throughout all streaming plaforms to 2025, the goal behind Dark Axolotl is to help artists create their dreamt songs a reality by being part of the creative process through production, mixing, mastering (We are proudly an Apple Digital Masters certified studio) and even bringing the song to immersive formats like Dolby Atmos, Eclipsa Audio & Sony 360 Reality Audio.

Services

Here are listed the services that Dark Axolotl offers.

Music Production

Take your music from an idea to a reality with our Music Production service, crafting unique sounds, arrangements, and textures to shape your song into a fully realized masterpiece.

Stereo Mixing & Mastering (inc. Apple Digital Masters)

Get your desired sound with the Stereo Mixing & Mastering process, delivering crystal-clear sound, depth, and balance for a polished listening experience. Featuring Apple Digital Masters to ensure the best possible quality on Apple Music and iTunes Store and getting a badge displayed on the album or single page on this stores.

Dolby Atmos, Eclipsa Audio & Sony 360 Reality Audio Immersive Mixing & Mastering

Take your song to the next dimension with a Dolby Atmos (available on Apple Music, Amazon Music and Tidal), Eclipsa Audio (available on YouTube) and Sony 360 Reality Audio (available on Amazon Music & Bandlab) Immersive Audio Mix & Master, with audio that surrounds you from all sides and above.
