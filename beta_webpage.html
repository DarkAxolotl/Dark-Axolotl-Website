Behind Dark Axolotl

Ton Cordero

Founder, Music Producer, Mix & Mastering Engineer

Full credits in Muso.AI

Dark Axolotl is a Mexico City based company specialized in music production, stereo and immersive mixing & mastering and other audio services. The goal behind Dark Axolotl is to help artists make their dream songs a reality by being part of the creative process through production, mixing, mastering (We are proudly an Apple Digital Masters certified studio) and even bringing the song to immersive formats like Dolby Atmos & Eclipsa Audio.

Services

Here are listed the services that Dark Axolotl offers.

Music Production

Take your music from an idea to a reality with our Music Production service, crafting unique sounds, arrangements, and textures to shape your song into a fully realized masterpiece.

Stereo Mixing & Mastering (inc. Apple Digital Masters)

Get your desired sound with the Stereo Mixing & Mastering process, delivering crystal-clear sound, depth, and balance for a polished listening experience. Now including Apple Digital Masters.

Dolby Atmos Immersive Mixing & Mastering

Take your song to the next dimension with a Dolby Atmos (available on Apple Music, Amazon Music and Tidal) & Eclipsa Audio (available on YouTube) Immersive Audio Mix & Master, with audio that surrounds you from all sides and above.
